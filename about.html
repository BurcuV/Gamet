How Was İt Start
This project, which was considered by seeing the lack of a comprehensive site for players in Turkey in the middle of 2021, turned into a concrete project at the end of 2021. The project, which wants to give players more information about the game world, was put forward with the idea of ​​increasing the number of gamers by talking about new ideas and games.
Mission
To be a brand that is closely followed by the game world, offers game promotion and opportunities, and is open to development every day.
Vision
To be a global game-talking brand where players can talk about games and benefit from opportunities.
